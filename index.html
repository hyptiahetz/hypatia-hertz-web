The Frequency of Another Universe

By blending electronic soundscapes with the rich tones of Turkish music, Ihsan Enes Akcinar pulls the listener into another universe—a dream realm where boundaries disappear. The texture he creates with powerful analog synths and nostalgic melodies reflects the deep melancholy of the human soul.

Hailing from Istanbul, the artist draws his creativity from the obscure line between silence and chaos, masterfully fusing cinematic darkness with the organic components of the lands he grew up in.
